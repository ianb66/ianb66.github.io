Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your computer or phone. They help websites:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use the site
  • Keep you logged in
  • Provide a better experience

Think of them like a bookmark that helps the website recognise you next time.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies on ianjbarwell.com to:

  • Remember if you accepted our cookie notice
  • Understand which pages are popular
  • See how visitors move through the site
  • Improve our website based on usage

We DO NOT use cookies to:

  • Track you across other websites
  • Sell your data
  • Store sensitive personal information

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

Purpose: Make the website work properly

Duration: Session or up to 1 year

Examples:

  • Cookie consent preferences
  • Security features
  • Basic website functionality

Can you disable them? No - the site won't work properly without these

2. Analytics Cookies

Purpose: Help us understand how visitors use our site

Duration: Usually up to 2 years

Examples:

  • Google Analytics (if used)
  • Page visit counts
  • Time spent on pages
  • Device and browser information

Can you disable them? Yes - see "How to Control Cookies" below

3. Performance Cookies

Purpose: Make the website faster and better

Duration: Session to 1 year

Examples:

  • Loading speed optimisation
  • Content delivery network (CDN) cookies

Can you disable them? Yes - but the site may be slower

How to Control Cookies

Option 1: Cookie Consent Banner

  • When you first visit, we'll ask about cookies
  • You can accept or decline non-essential cookies
  • You can change your choice anytime

Option 2: Browser Settings

Google Chrome:

  • Go to Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Choose your preference
  • You can block or delete specific cookies

Firefox:

  • Go to Settings > Privacy & Security
  • Under Cookies and Site Data, choose your setting
  • Click "Manage Data" to remove specific cookies

Safari:

  • Go to Preferences > Privacy
  • Choose "Block all cookies" or manage individually

What Happens If You Disable Cookies?

If you disable cookies:

  • Essential cookies disabled: The website may not function properly
  • Analytics cookies disabled: We can't improve the site based on usage data
  • Performance cookies disabled: The site might load slower
